Role Selection

In this section you can see which lane will best fit you.



Top Lane

Referring to the top lane, surely all of us think of strong and assertive gamers. The top lane is always the solo match of individuals who know how to shine and they are like the front line of a battle.

Mid Lane

Mid is always the role that many gamers love because there will be a lot of acting places with highly skilled people.Mid is often those who have creativity, patience and hard work.

ADC (Bottom)

If you are a person who has restraint and patience, you will be very suitable for the ADC position.To play ADC, you have to work hard to farm creeps, and also have to be patient before the underdogs. Sometimes the support is not very good, they must also be patient to give advantage to the team in the latter stages of the match. Because AD is the position of the late game, let’s practice how to be a real ADC!

Support (Bottom)

If you want to be influential support, you possess the following characteristics: sociable, happy, giving in and sacrificing for your teammates.

Jungle

As a jungler, you will have to gank a lot and that makes you pay attention to all the lane. The position you take is extremely important and you must have a smart brain to be able to judge the situation of the team.Not only that but with Jungle the support of teammates also needs regular interaction, like “my teammates call, I’m here”. Because of that, a person must be a jungler who is sociable, enthusiastic, always knows how to help others.
